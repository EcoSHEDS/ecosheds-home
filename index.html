What is SHEDS?

SHEDS is a web application that seamlessly links hydro-ecological datasets, models, and decision support systems.

The goal is to develop tools for gaining insight, improving decision making, and supporting better management of hydro-ecological resources.

Datasets

  • Stream Temperature Database
  • High Resolution Catchment Delineation
  • Fish Database
  • Culvert Database

Models

The databases are fed into predictive models.

  • Stream Temperature
  • Fish Occupancy

Decision Tools

  • Interactive Catchment Explorer
